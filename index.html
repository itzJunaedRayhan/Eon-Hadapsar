About

Eon Hadapsar Infrastructure Private Limited is a real estate development company that is engaged in the construction, development, and sale of high-end residential projects.

A wholly owned subsidiary of the USD 0.2 billion organisation Panchshil Realty, Eon Hadapsar Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated as a private limited company and limited by shares under the Indian Companies Act, 1956 on August 31, 2004. The non-convertible debentures issued by the Company on private placement basis are listed on the debt segment of BSE Limited.

Eon Hadapsar Infrastructure Private Limited is headquartered in Pune, with its registered and corporate offices located in Yerwada.

Projects

One North

Spread over 11 acres of tranquil premises, One North stands tall amid an alluring expanse of 70% green landscaping. At One North, the grass will always be greener on your side!

Here, you can expect the most luxurious living experience, with homes that are truly exclusive and exquisite. With 4-bedroom apartments ranging between sizes 3,200 sq. ft. to 4,600 sq. ft. and penthouses of 5,600 sq. ft., One North brings a whole new meaning to plush living.

These limited edition high-rise residences welcome you to a world of your own where you can sink into the sheer luxury of open spaces! Award-winning interior designer Twinkle Khanna also adds her quintessential celebrity style to the apartments at One North, to enable you to create your own indulgent living space.
